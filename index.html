Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 9, 2025

App Name: МаршрутПро

Developer: Akbarov Akbar Nabijonovich

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the "МаршрутПро" mobile application ("the App") collects, uses, and protects user data. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

2. Data Collection

The App does not collect or store any personal data from users. Specifically:

  • No registration or login is required.
  • No name, phone number, email, or other personal details are requested.
  • No photos, files, or messages are accessed.

3. Location Data

The App may access your device's location (with your permission) in order to:

  • Show your current location on the map.
  • Help you find nearby bus routes or stops.

Location data is used only within the app and is not stored or shared with any external server or third party.

4. Offline and Cached Data

To improve performance, the App stores route and stop data locally on your device. This data includes:

  • Bus stop coordinates and names
  • Route paths and segments
  • Previously viewed data

No user-related data is cached.

5. Internet Access

The App may access the internet to:

  • Download updated route and stop data
  • Load maps (from services like OpenStreetMap)
  • Check for app updates or display news (optional)

This usage is strictly technical and does not involve user identification.

6. Developer Mode

The App includes a Developer Mode intended for internal use only and not available to regular users. Access to this mode is restricted by a login and password. It allows authorized developers to manage and edit route and stop data. No personal user data is collected or processed in Developer Mode.

7. Ads and Analytics

The App does not contain ads and does not use any analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or Firebase).

8. Third-Party Services

The App may use external services such as:

  • OpenStreetMap – for displaying maps.

These services are used only to visualize public map data and do not receive any user data from the App.

9. Data Security

We do not collect any personal information; therefore, no personal data is at risk. All cached technical data is stored securely on the user's device.

10. Advertising ID

The App uses the device's Advertising ID solely for displaying ads. This identifier may be accessed by included advertising SDKs (such as AdMob) to provide personalized or contextual advertising. While the App itself does not process this ID directly, the SDK may use it for ad-related purposes. Please note: some SDKs may also perform limited analytics using the Advertising ID. The App does not actively collect or analyze such data.

11. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages and complies with COPPA. It does not contain content inappropriate for children. However, children under 13 should use the App under parental supervision, especially if location access is enabled.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are made, the updated version will be available at the official link provided in the app's store listing.
